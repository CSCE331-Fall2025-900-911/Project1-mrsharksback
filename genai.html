Generative AI: What It Can Do for Game Teams

  • Rapid ideation: mechanics, pillars, pitch pages.
  • Procedural worldbuilding under constraints (maps, dungeons, encounters).
  • Narrative support: branching dialog drafts, quest variations, NPC barks.
  • Systems tuning: parameter sweeps, economy balancing suggestions.
  • Asset placeholders: concept art, SFX ideas, VFX prompts.
  • QA & playtesting: synthetic runs, bug summaries, test mission generation.
  • Documentation: design notes, patch‑note drafts, change logs.
  • Tooling: natural‑language commands to trigger editor pipelines.

Tip: Constrain generation with your world bible, style guide, and gameplay rules.
